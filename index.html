Join Hands To Eradicate Hunger From India

AAHAR is a volunteer based organization that works to collect surplus food from college messes to serve the less fortunate people. Contribute your bit in resolving a crisis that has 200 million Indians in itâ€™s grip.

Process of AAHAR

1

Food is saved

Surplus food is collected after lunch (Around 200 kgs of unutilised food is wasted daily in the institute messes)

2

Drive is conducted

Food is transported using E-rickshaws (More than 100 drives have been conducted since August 2017)

3

Food is distributed

Food is served in nearby slums (More than 6000 people have been fed with this surplus food)
